# 📊 Ecommerce Analytics Portfolio (SQL + Power BI)

This repository contains multiple **end-to-end ecommerce analytics projects**, demonstrating **data engineering, SQL, Power BI dashboarding, and business insights**.

The goal of this portfolio is to show how raw data can be transformed into actionable insights to drive **strategic business decisions**.

---

## 📁 Repository Structure

### 1️⃣ 1_Online_Stores_Analysis (Super Store Sales)
**Focus:** Business insights & dashboard storytelling  

- Cleaned and normalized US retail data (2014–2017) using SQL  
- Performed **financial calculations**: gross/net revenue, discount analysis, per-unit metrics  
- Built **interactive Power BI dashboards** with KPIs, charts, and maps  
- Key insights:
  - High discounts reduced profitability (up to 60% in some cases)  
  - Top customers and regions contributed most to revenue  
  - Category-level analysis identified loss-making products  

---

### 2️⃣ 2_Global_Electronics_Analysis ⭐⭐⭐ *(Recommended Project)*
**Focus:** End-to-end analytics + business strategy  

- Cleaned and standardized multi-table datasets: customers, sales, products, stores, exchange rates  
- Created **SQL analytical views** for revenue, orders, discounts, and customer/product insights  
- Deep analysis highlights:
  - US market dominance (~44% customers) → high regional dependency  
  - Computers category drives highest revenue and profit  
  - Strong seasonality (Dec–Feb peak) and revenue concentration among few products/customers  
  - Some stores underperforming → opportunity for optimization  
- Built **Power BI dashboards** with dynamic filtering, KPIs, and bookmarks  
- Delivered **actionable recommendations**: market expansion, pricing, discount strategy, and customer targeting  

---

### 3️⃣ 3_SQL_PBI_Retail_Sales_Analytics
**Focus:** Technical implementation & data modeling  

- Imported, cleaned, and transformed raw datasets using SQL  
- Created **fact & dimension tables** and analytical SQL views  
- Designed **Power BI star schema** with calculated measures and advanced DAX  
- Metrics & KPIs:
  - Total Customers, Products, Orders, Quantity  
  - Gross Sales, Net Sales, Average Discount Rate  
  - Advanced calculations using `CALCULATE()` and `TREATAS()`  
- Built dashboards for customer insights, product performance, and order analysis

## 🚀 Business Insights Across Projects

- Revenue concentrated in few high-performing products and regions  
- Older customer demographics dominate; youth segment underrepresented
